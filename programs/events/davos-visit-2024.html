YAIL Delegation to Davos 2024

Munich Hub members represented the youth perspective at AI House Davos through plenary sessions, consultations, and collaborative outputs.

Lebkuchen, #SharedPassion, and the first #YAIL spark

December 2023: we asked, “Why don’t we go to Davos #WEF?” The answer was simple,AI House Davos. We showed up with a slick #ONEPAGER, met founders Alex Ilic and Andreas Gillhuber, and launched the Young AI Leaders movement with a ton of support.

We brought a 15+ member delegation to Davos 2024, showing that Young AI Leaders care about practical prototypes and policy conversations at AI House.

Let’s see what 2025 holds,happy Advent and keep the hashtags alive (#Passion #Community #YAIL).
