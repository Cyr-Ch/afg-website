Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Munich Hub?

Fill out our membership application form above or send us an email. We welcome individuals aged 18-30 who are passionate about using AI for social good.

Do I need AI experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome members from diverse backgrounds. Our community includes beginners, experts, and everyone in between. We provide learning opportunities for all levels.

What kind of events do you organize?

We host workshops, panel discussions, hackathons, networking events, and project showcases. Check our Programs & Events page for upcoming activities.

How can my organization partner with you?

We're always looking for partnerships with universities, companies, and NGOs. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities, sponsorships, or joint events.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership in AI for Good Munich Hub is completely free. We believe in making our community accessible to everyone.

Can I propose a project idea?

Absolutely! We encourage members to propose project ideas that align with the SDGs. We'll help you find collaborators and resources to bring your vision to life.
