About Us

Empowering the next generation of AI leaders in Munich

Our Mission

To empower young professionals aged 18-30 in Munich to leverage artificial intelligence for positive social impact, fostering innovation that addresses the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals while building a vibrant community of changemakers.

  • Connect diverse young talent with AI expertise
  • Facilitate collaboration on SDG-focused projects
  • Provide mentorship and learning opportunities
  • Bridge the gap between innovation and social good

Our Vision

A world where AI for Good leaders from Munich are at the forefront of technological innovation for global good, creating sustainable solutions that transform communities and inspire the next generation of responsible AI development.

  • Lead technological innovation for global good
  • Create sustainable solutions that transform communities
  • Inspire the next generation of responsible AI leaders
  • Position Munich at the forefront of AI for social impact
